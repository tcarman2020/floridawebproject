Florida

"The Sunshine State"

Home Tallahassee Jacksonville Tampa Orlando Miami
  • Florida is located in the southeastern region of the United States and it's Capital is Tallahassee.
  • It is almost completely surrounded by water, bordering with the Gulf of Mexico on the west and the Atlantic Ocean to the east, classifying it as a Penninsula.
  • It is the 3rd most populated State with over 22,000,000 residents.
  • The climate is subtropical/tropical with an average of 85.58% humidity ranking it 4th in the U.S.
  • Florida holds the most popular theme parks and water parks in the country including:
    1. Disney World
    2. Universal & Islands of Adventure
    3. Sea World
    4. LEGOLAND
    5. Busch Gardens.
    6. Blizzard Beach & Typhoon Lagoon
  • It is also home to tons of wildlife.
  • The Everglades National Park is the only place in the world where crocodiles and alligators co-exist.
